Pan Sauce

  • 1 cup Chicken Stock
  • Optional addition of cooking wine
  • 1-2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 whole shallot minced
  • half small lemon for juice
  • 2 tbls of cold butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. After cooking your choice of meat (chicken, beef, pork) add shallots to same pan, if needed add small amount of cooking oil
  2. Once shallots have gained some color add garlic
  3. Once shallots and garlic have good color and are fragrent, add chicken stock
  4. Allow the stock to reduce at a simmer for about 6-8 min
  5. Once reduced, add butter
  6. Squeeze lemon, and season to taste, checking as you go
  7. Eat with previously mentioned meat, enjoy!
